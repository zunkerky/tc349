About Us

In 2009 DeWitt Computer Center was founded in DeWitt, Michigan, just north of Lansing. Since then we have been providing high quality computer repair and support services to local DeWitt and Lansing area residents. We pride ourselves in being able to provide support to a wide range of IT topics that todays residential computer and technology users face. Please take a look at our Services page to view our official service and price offerings. If there is a specific need that is not listed don't hesistate to call us. Our staff solves new and challenging problems everyday and can help point you in the right direction with any IT related need.
