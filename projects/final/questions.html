Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What do I need to bring in for my computer repair?

A: For desktop computer repair please bring in just the desktop tower. For laptop repair please bring in the laptop along with the power cord.

Q: How long do repairs take?

A: Repair time is very dependent on what the specific problem is. For most repairs it typically takes 2 to 4 days, it may take longer if parts need to be ordered.

Q: What is the best Anti-Virus software?

A: There is no perfect Anti-Virus software. We have found that some of the available free software will work just as well as the paid Anti-Virus software. We reccommend Avast Free Anti-Virus or if you would like to pay for Anti-Virus software we recommend GDATA or Kaspersky.

Q: How often should I backup my data?

A: There is no perfect timeframe to backup data on a computer, we reccommend that a backup is made anytime important files have been added or changes have been made to a computer.

Q: Do you repair printers?

A: We do repair software related issues with printers, but have found that for most printers if it is physically damaged it is cheaper to replace the printer rather than having it repaired.
